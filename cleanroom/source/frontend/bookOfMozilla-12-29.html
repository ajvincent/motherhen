Indeed, the day is upon us when the Beast shall reside with the Hen, following the birds of Thunder and the monkeys of the Sea. Yea, even the Griffons of the Blue realm would smile upon this day. Weep not for the years and Decades which have passed in Darkness. I tell you, the Hen shall raise Many chicks for the Beast, and among them, the Runner shall be Reborn to run free Forever...
